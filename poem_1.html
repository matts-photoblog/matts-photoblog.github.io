Frosted Feelings

A frosted lawn covers dead sentience —
But where does feeling go
when it no longer grows?

God, we are missing you,
buried under soil,
our consciousness gone and,
fed upon by the money worms.

Sunshine freezes through time and space —
But where does light flow
when existence must go?
